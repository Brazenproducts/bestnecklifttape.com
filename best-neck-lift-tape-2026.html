Application Tips for Best Results

  1. Start with clean, dry skin. Remove all moisturizer, sunscreen, and oils. Use an alcohol-free toner to prep the area.
  2. Find your angle. Use a mirror and gently pull the skin up and back until you find the lift you want. That's where the tape goes.
  3. Apply behind the ear. Most tapes work best when anchored behind or below the ear, pulling the neck skin up and back.
  4. Press firmly for 30 seconds. Body heat activates the adhesive. Don't rush this step.
  5. Apply makeup AFTER taping. Use a damp beauty sponge to blend foundation over the tape edges.
  6. Remove gently. Use an oil-based cleanser or coconut oil to dissolve the adhesive. Never rip tape off dry skin.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does neck lift tape last?

Most quality neck lift tapes last 8-12 hours with proper application. Factors like humidity, sweating, and skin type can affect wear time. Applying to clean, dry, oil-free skin maximizes adhesion.

Can you wear neck lift tape under makeup?

Yes, the best neck lift tapes are designed to be worn under makeup. Apply the tape first to clean skin, then apply foundation and concealer over the edges. Matte-finish tapes work best under makeup.

Is neck lift tape safe for sensitive skin?

Most neck lift tapes use medical-grade adhesive that's safe for sensitive skin. However, if you have very reactive skin, look for hypoallergenic options like Blumbody and do a patch test first. Remove tape gently with oil-based remover to avoid irritation.

How do you apply neck lift tape properly?

Start with clean, dry skin free of oils and moisturizer. Pull the skin up and back to your desired position, then apply the tape to hold it in place. Most tapes work best when placed behind the ear or along the jawline. Practice makes perfect — most people get the hang of it after 3-4 applications.

Can men use neck lift tape?

Absolutely. Neck lift tape works on all skin types regardless of gender. Men may need to shave the application area for better adhesion, and placement behind the ear works well since it's hidden by the hairline.
